Specialist Periodontist

Dr. Srihari

MDS Periodontology BDS Practo Verified 100/100 Rating

Dr. Srihari is a highly respected Specialist Periodontist and Dentist at S.N Dental Clinic in R.S. Puram, Coimbatore. With over 25 years of dedicated clinical experience, he has established himself as one of Coimbatore's most trusted dental specialists.

Specializing in periodontal surgery, dental implants, and comprehensive restorative dentistry, Dr. Srihari combines scientific precision with a compassionate, patient-first approach. He takes great pride in explaining treatment options clearly and ensuring every patient feels comfortable and confident.
