Dental Implants

Dental implants are titanium posts surgically placed into the jawbone to replace missing tooth roots. They provide a permanent, stable foundation for crowns, bridges, or dentures — looking, feeling, and functioning just like natural teeth.

  • Permanent & natural-looking solution
  • Prevents bone loss in the jaw
  • No special maintenance required
  • Can last a lifetime with proper care
Procedure Steps:
1
Initial consultation & X-ray / CT scan assessment
2
Titanium implant placement under local anaesthesia
3
Healing period (osseointegration) — 3–6 months
4
Crown/bridge placement on the implant

Dr. Srihari's Specialty

Periodontal Surgery

As an MDS Periodontologist, Dr. Srihari specializes in treating advanced gum disease. Periodontal flap surgery removes deep calculus deposits and corrects bone defects to restore healthy gums and prevent tooth loss.

  • Treats advanced gum disease
  • Prevents tooth loss
  • Reduces bleeding and inflammation
  • Specialist-level precision
Procedure Steps:
1
Periodontal probing and X-ray assessment
2
Scaling and root planing (deep cleaning)
3
Flap surgery to access deep bone deposits (if needed)
4
Suturing and post-operative care guidance
